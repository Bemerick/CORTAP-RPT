## Deficiencies Found

### 1. Technical Capacity – Award Management (TC-AM5-1)

**Deficiency:** Inactive award/untimely closeouts

**Details:**
- 11 inactive grants
- 5 awards on the closeout list
- Award closeout retainage issues
- Does not initiate closeout within 120 days once activity is completed

**Corrective Actions Required:**
1. Submit more effective procedures for award management to enable award closeout
2. Submit an award closeout plan for all awards

**Response Due Date:** October 30, 2023
**Closure Date:** August 23, 2023

**Closure Documentation:** Submitted revised closeout schedule that aligns with ACCT 45 FTA Retainage Release for Grant Closeout procedure (submitted August 18, 2023)

### 2. Satisfactory Continuing Control (SCC8-4)

**Deficiency:** No evidence of physical inventory or reconciliation

**Details:**
- SAP asset listing includes individual assets and project-level line items
- Departments maintain separate inventories that may be more detailed
- Physical inventory memo developed by PATCO is at project and award-level only
- Memo does not provide details at asset level to conduct reconciliation with SAP assets
- No documentation of reconciliation between list of assets in SAP and physical assets

**Corrective Actions Required:**
1. Submit written inventory control procedures defining roles and responsibilities for tracking FTA-funded assets and reconciling physical inventories to equipment records
2. Submit a timeline for performing the physical inventory and reconciliation of FTA-funded equipment

**Response Due Date:** October 30, 2023
**Closure Date:** August 7, 2023

**Closure Documentation:** Submitted ACCT 44 FTA Asset and Equipment Reconciliation and SOP 8. DRPA will complete next inventory by December 2024.

### 3. Procurement (P11-1)

**Deficiency:** Missing FTA Clauses

**Details:** DRPA procurement files do not include:
1. Notice to FTA and U.S. DOT Inspector General of information related to fraud, waste, abuse, or other legal matters
2. Prohibition on certain telecommunications and video surveillance services or equipment
3. Seatbelt use per FTA Master Agreement Section 34(a)
4. Distracted driving per FTA Master Agreement Section 34(a)

**Corrective Actions Required:**
1. Submit revised procurement procedures that address inclusion of all FTA-required third-party contract clauses through use of a clause checklist or other mechanism
2. Submit a procedure for updating the required contract clauses on an annual basis
3. Submit evidence of implementation with the next applicable procurement

**Response Due Dates:**
- October 30, 2023 (procedures)
- December 11, 2023 (evidence of implementation)

**Closure Date:** August 7, 2023

**Closure Documentation:** Submitted revised Procurement Manual addressing the corrective action. Bid number 12-K was released in July 2023 with all applicable clauses.

### 4. Title VI (TVI2-1)

**Deficiency:** Language Assistance Plan implementation deficiencies

**Details:**
- DRPA Language Assistance Plan notes that training is provided "on policies and procedures concerning language assistance and ways to determine whether an individual needs assistance services" through new employee orientation process
- DRPA is not providing this training during orientation
- Title VI Training not conducted with new hire orientation or on language line per Title VI Program LAP

**Corrective Actions Required:**
Prepare and submit to FTA Office of Civil Rights documentation of implementing its LAP, including the completion of required training

**Response Due Date:** October 30, 2023
**Closure Date:** August 22, 2023

**Closure Documentation:** New Hire Orientation was completed August 22, 2023, including the Language Assistance Plan presentation.
