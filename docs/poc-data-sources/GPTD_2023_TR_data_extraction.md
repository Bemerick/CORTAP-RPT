## Deficiency Summary

**Total Deficiencies:** 3 (2 closed, 1 open at report date)

**Deficiency Details:**

### Deficiency 1: P11-1 (CLOSED)
- **Review Area:** Procurement
- **Code:** P11-1
- **Description:** Missing FTA clauses. In the purchase of New Flyer buses awarded March 31, 2022, the required clause regarding prohibition of certain telecommunications and video surveillance services or equipment (2 CFR 200.216, effective August 13, 2020) was missing.
- **Corrective Action:** By November 30, 2023, GPTD must submit revised procurement procedures with clause checklist and demonstrate implementation.
- **Status:** CLOSED June 26, 2023 after GPTD provided revised checklist and documentation of implementation

### Deficiency 2: DBE5-1 (CLOSED)
- **Review Area:** Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE)
- **Code:** DBE5-1
- **Description:** DBE uniform reports contain inaccuracies. GPTD incorrectly reported revenue contract funds (30% retained by DBE advertising firm from revenue-sharing agreement) as DBE awards/payments in semi-annual reports. Revenue contracts cannot be counted toward DBE attainment.
- **Corrective Action:** By November 30, 2023, GPTD must submit corrected DBE Uniform Reports for 2020-2022, procedures for correctly completing reports, and new DBE goal with revised methodology.
- **Status:** CLOSED June 28, 2023 after GPTD submitted corrected reports, procedures, and revised DBE goal

### Deficiency 3: TVI4-1 (OPEN)
- **Review Area:** Title VI
- **Code:** TVI4-1
- **Description:** Title VI complaint process not implemented in accordance with Title VI Program. Per 49 CFR Section 21.9(b), recipients must develop procedures for investigating and tracking Title VI complaints. GPTD's Title VI Plan states investigations will be completed within 60 days, but four complaints received in 2022 were not resolved within this timeframe.
- **Corrective Action:** By December 31, 2023, GPTD must submit evidence of complaint processing per Title VI Program. By August 31, 2023, GPTD must submit revised Title VI Program correctly describing complaint process.
- **Due Date:** August 31, 2023 / December 31, 2023
- **Status:** Open (no closure date shown in report)
